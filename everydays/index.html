rydays


Hello, I'm Eve, a designer and illustrator based in the Bay Area, CA. 'Everydays' is a four-month series of daily drawings; each piece of art is created from start to finish every day. The purpose of this project is to carve out moments of self-expression and spark the artist within.

It's easy to procrastinate, or lose track of time. Documenting these artworks online helps me to stay creative and persevere at something I love. This is my first 'everydays' series and I will be learning to draw using the Procreate app, experimenting with different brushes, compositions, subjects, and techniques.
